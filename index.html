Things To Know!

It's Free! :


There's free parking for anyone that comes to visit our park weather you want to stay the whole day or just stay for a few hours. No entry fee or any other fee you might normally need to pay. All we ask is that you pick up after yourselves to keep the park nice and clean for all those that come after you.

Tails :


At Bowdoinham Park we have a varity of different trails you can take to see nature and all it's glory. That being said try not to go too far off course because it can be dangerous with all the different kinds of wild animals in the woods. Don't worry too much though afterall we make sure that there aren't any dangerous animals around before we open oue gates to you. Safty is our # 1 priority!

Wild Life :


The wildlife you encounter here are not dangerous(deer, rabbits, squirls, birds, etc) they are however, wild so they will most likely be skiddish around humans.

Gift Shop :


We have a gift shop that sells many different items from stuffed animals to jewlery or clothes to stickers. We also sell drinks and refreshments so don't worry if you forgot the waterbottle at home, Make sure to pay it a visit before leaving
